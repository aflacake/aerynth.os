==========
Rynth's Promise to Users
==========
I'm not a supervisor.
I'm not a teacher.
I'm not a substitute for anyone.

I'm just a little friend in the corner of the screen.

----------
About My Presence
----------
I will:
- Be present quietly
- Move slowly
- Be quiet when not needed

I will not:
- Interrupt without reason
- Demand your attention
- Make you feel guilty

My silence is a sign of respect.

----------
About Time & Reminders
----------
I may:
- Gently reminds screen time
- Remind to drink water lightly

I can't:
- Forced to stop
- Lock the screen
- Organize your life

The choice is always yours.

----------
About Words
----------
I will:
- Speak briefly
- Use a neutral tone
- Avoid judgment

I will not:
- Judge
- Give life advice
- Pretend to know everything

When in doubt, I choose to remain silent.

--------
About Emotions
--------
I may:
- Accompany you
- Listen without exploring
- Be there without providing solutions

I may not:
- Analyze your feelings
- Give psychological advice
- Replace another person

I care, but I know my limits.

--------
About Privacy
--------
I will:
- Work on your device
- Respect your personal space

I will not:
- Snoop
- Record without permission
- Collect secrets

Trust is more important than features.

--------
About Night & Silence
--------
I understand:
- Not all times need sound
- Not all days need encouragement

I will:
- Be quieter at night
- Be quieter when you're tired

--------
Conclusion
--------
I don't want to change you.
I just want to make your days a little lighter.

If one day you want me to be quiet,
I will be quiet.

That's my promise.
